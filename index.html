Wyoming

A 2015 legal settlement requires Wyoming regulators to more carefully scrutinize claims by oil and gas drilling companies that the ingredients of fracking fluids are trade secrets. If a trade secret exemption is granted, chemical information disclosed to the Wyoming Oil and Gas Conversation Commission will not be publicly available. Source
