Tracking Fracking

The laws that govern disclosure of chemicals injected deep underground during hydraulic fracturing operations vary from state to state. Recent federal action established rules for drilling on federal lands, but the rest of the U.S. is covered piecemeal at the state level. Click your state to learn more about its rules.

Wyoming

A 2015 legal settlement requires Wyoming regulators to more carefully scrutinize claims by oil and gas drilling companies that the ingredients of fracking fluids are trade secrets. If a trade secret exemption is granted, chemical information disclosed to the Wyoming Oil and Gas Conversation Commission will not be publicly available. Source
